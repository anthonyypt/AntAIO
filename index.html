Trackr

Order & Inventory Management Software

Trackr is a modern desktop application for tracking online orders, managing inventory, and scraping order confirmation emails from Gmail.
Designed for collectors, resellers, and anyone who wants to keep their order and inventory data organized in one place.

  • Order Tracking & Management
  • Inventory Management
  • Gmail Email Scraping (secure, OAuth2-based)
  • Privacy-focused: all data stored locally
  • License key activation (coming soon)
