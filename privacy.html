Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-07-13

TrackrIO ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our software.

1. Information We Collect

  • Order and Inventory Data: Information you enter or import into the app, such as order numbers, inventory items, and related details.
  • Gmail Data: If you connect your Gmail account, we access order confirmation emails to extract order numbers, dates, and amounts. We do not access or store your email content beyond what is necessary for order tracking.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide order tracking and inventory management features.
  • To extract and display order information from your Gmail account (if enabled).

3. Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your data to any external servers.
  • We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to third parties.

4. Data Security

  • We use OAuth2 authentication for secure access to your Gmail account.
  • Your Gmail credentials are never stored or shared.

5. License and Activation

  • Complete Tracker is a commercial product and may require a license key for activation.
